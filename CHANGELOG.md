## [1.0.0] - 2021-02-19
### Added
- Support for the following GitLab webhook events:
  - `merge requests`
  - `job`
  - `pipeline`
  - `push`
  - `tag push`
  - `comment`
  - `deployment`
  - `feature flag`
  - `issue`
  - `release`
  - `wiki page`
